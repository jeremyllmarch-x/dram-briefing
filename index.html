HBM4 16-Hi Race

Nvidia demands 16-Hi HBM4 from all 3 vendors by Q4 2026 — wafer thinning from 50μm to 30μm the key challenge

Nvidia has formally requested SK Hynix, Samsung, and Micron deliver 16-Hi HBM4 stacks by Q4 2026. Stacking 16 layers requires thinning each die from ~50μm (current 12-Hi) to ~30μm — a critical process engineering challenge that determines yield and cost. 16-Hi format delivers ~33% more capacity per package. All three vendors are in full-scale development; first to qualify at volume gets preferential Nvidia allocation.
